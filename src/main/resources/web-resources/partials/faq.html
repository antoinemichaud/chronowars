Chronowars, the rules !


Placements
You and your opponent each will place 5 token, only on your own color.
How to move
You can then move your tokens, still on your own color, either by one tile diagonally, or two tiles by jumping over an opponent token.
The way to win
Use your and your opponent tokens to form shapes in order to earn points, reach 15 points to win the game. The player who completes the shape earns the points corresponding to it
One shape by turn
A single tile cannot be use to be part of two shapes in a single turn (e.g, ooo being a shape, a player forming oooo will only complete one shape)
